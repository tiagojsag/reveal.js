Agile Principles

"Business people and developers must work together daily throughout the project."

  • Communication is key - inside and outside the team
  • Learn from and teach your team mates
  • Talk with customers - it helps you and them

Agile

"Welcome changing requirements, even late in development. Agile processes harness change for the customer's competitive advantage."

  • Customers change their minds - and that's ok
  • Prepare for change with good communication and tech practices

Agile

"Build projects around motivated individuals. Give them the environment and support they need, and trust them to get the job done."

  • Rely on your team - they are there for you
  • If you need something, ask

Agile

"Working software is the primary measure of progress."

  • Customers want working software
  • All other metrics are secondary

Agile

"At regular intervals, the team reflects on how to become more effective, then tunes and adjusts its behavior accordingly."

  • Communication is key - give and accept feedback
  • Do be afraid to be honest - if you give honest feedback, you will be sure you are getting honest feedback
